Hi I'm Josie :D

a multidisciplinary creative currently pursuing a degree in Industrial Design at the National University of Singapore (NUS). I find all aspects of design fascinating and love dabbling in its many fields, from art directing advertising campaigns to motion graphics and UX/UI. I enjoy pushing myself to create, drawing inspiration from experiences and the people around me, as I value learning a thing or two from others.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me hoarding cute food packaging, adopting stuffed toys, or sipping way too much green tea (it’s a lifestyle).
