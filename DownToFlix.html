Netflix: Down To Flix?

D&AD Brief 2022: Netflix UK

Passion Project • 2022

The Ask

Netflix is a global streaming entertainment service offering ad free, unlimited viewing focused on movies and TV series. Despite this broad language offering, UK consumer appetite for non-English language titles has historically been low. Netflix believe that great stories are universal. They want to show members around the world that great content can come from anywhere, and be loved by anyone.

The Challenge

Create excitement for non-English language content amongst UK audiences, and leverage this as a conversation driver among fans of entertainment. Develop a campaign that gets more UK audiences to consider, explore, try watching and talk about non-English language films and series on Netflix.

Campaign Case Film

Forget Netflix’s endless list of dating shows, we have a special dating experience for you. Despite their wide range of films, many foreign ones still go unnoticed. However, we found that Netflix is full of films, waiting to be loved. In addition, films have now become a form of comfort through shared experiences. So to introduce more UK citizens to the world of non-english films and other cultures around the world, we created the campaign “Down to Flix?”. By personifying the films, you can now choose dates from all over the world in our virtual motel. Perfect for those who want some extra loving during cuffing season. So, Are You Down to Flix?
