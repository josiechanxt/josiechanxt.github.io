Mandai X Ranger Buddies

UX/UI B2C Edutainment Research 2026

"How might we design a seamless, 'phygital' edutainment experience that integrates educational value with engaging play for children?”

Ranger Buddies is an edutainment initiative by Mandai X, the venture-building arm of the Mandai Wildlife Group. It is designed to nurture a generation of "everyday heroes" by engaging children in wildlife conservation and environmental action through a blend of digital and physical experiences. As a UI/UX design intern, I contributed to the B2C experience of the Ranger Buddies web app, focusing on in-app features, mission flows, and collaborative campaigns.

My Contributions

  • Spearheaded the validation of the web app's new reward system feature. Created high-fidelity prototypes, led physical User Testing sessions with Zoo visitors, and presented synthesised findings to the CEO to drive feature implementation

  • Created UI screens and graphic assets for digital mission games

  • Conducted live testing at the physical mission sites to ensure a seamless phygital experience

  • Conducted competitor research for a brand collaboration touchpoint, providing key insights that guided the Product Managers' strategic implementations.

    Validating the Reward System Feature

    I helped develop the brand-new reward system (MVP) for the RB web app. The goal was to incentivise kids for completing their missions, and access partner rewards in one key place. In only three weeks until rollout, I rapidly validated user flows, currency comprehension and navigation for two distinct user profiles: Parents and children.

    Phase 1: High-Fidelity Prototyping of Flows

    Below are the main flows/screens with the newly-added rewards elements:
    (For context: “Books” are being used for our main method of reward currency)

    ✏️ The "Mission Flow": A simulated 6-activity walkthrough designed to help users understand mission mechanics, where upon the completion of each activity, the new “Books” meter bar fills up by 100 points.

    ✏️ The "Redeem Rewards Flow": A new loop covering partner selection, profile options, and reward redemption process.

    ✏️ The "Home Page": Integrating a new “Books Menu” entry point directly onto the existing map interface homepage.

    Phase 2: Conducting On-Site User Testing at Mandai Wildlife Zoo

    We conducted rapid on-site guerilla user testing at Mandai Wildlife Zoo, capturing high-value, 15-minute sessions with visiting families. As the interviewer, I tailored the UT Guide for both parent and child demographics. Since the children were quite young (4-10 years old), I simplified the phrasing to ensure easier comprehension while avoiding leading questions for authentic responses. For example, after a progress bar is filled, I would ask: “What do you notice has changed? → Why do you think that happened?” to guide them along the process.

    Phase 3: Refinements, additional testing

    Afterwards, I synthesised the responses. In our first testing, there were some challenges that both parents and children faced. I refined the prototype, making sure the solutions address the issues, prominently, the drop-off after completing the mission.

    Phase 4: Testing against our core objectives

    We returned to the zoo to test the new iterations with more visitors against our core objectives.
