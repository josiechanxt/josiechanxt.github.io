Redesigning TikTok Location Service

UX/UI, user research

Passion Project • 2025

Project Brief

I gave myself a challenge to redesign a feature in one of my most commonly used app, Tik Tok, within 5 days, as I want to demonstrate my UX/UI skills despite not having many personal projects that showcase it. I looked to redesign the location service page as I felt that there is a lot of potential to explore.

  • Timeline: 5 Days

  • Collaborator(s): Individual

  • Key skills: UI/UX, user research, user testing, time management

    • "How might we redesign the consumer experience for TikTok Local Service?”

    TikTok is a global short-form video platform that empowers diverse communities to unleash their creativity and share authentic stories. Its algorithm-driven discovery quickly connects users to niche interests and genuine human connection, fostering a sense of belonging all around the world.

    Consumers → Creators

    It got me thinking, who are the consumers? While "consuming" feels more like "using," I prefer to think of the target audience as creators. This is a more fitting word because on TikTok, we not only consume information but also create experiences with others in the community.

    Going through the user journey

    To understand the user experience, I used the Tik Tok app to review the current interface, and put myself in the user's shoes, performing a click-through of the page to identify the necessary tasks and understand how it’s features works.

    I gave myself user tasks…

    1. Find location service page

    2. Read other creators' reviews and experiences about my favourite restaurant after seeing a video about it.

    User Testing

    Due to the limited time and resources, I drafted a quick UT discussion guide with hypotheses of user tasks I wanted to test, which is similar to my previous personal testing with similar user tasks, albeit with more questions added to better understand their thoughts and gain more insights.

    Goals of the User Testing

    1. Get general thoughts on how other TikTok users use the page

    2. Test out the user experience with the business details, reviews and location service features

    3. Identify unique TikTok experiences that I can capitalise on

    Synthesising data, finding insights

    I synthesised the data collected from user testing and identified key insights that could inform design decisions. This process involved analyzing user feedback, identifying patterns, and distilling findings into actionable recommendations.
