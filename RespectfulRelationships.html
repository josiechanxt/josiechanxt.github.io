Singapore Polytechnic Respectful Relationship series

Infographics

Client: Singapore Polytechnic • 2022

Project Brief

Freelanced for my alma mater, Singapore Polytechnic's School of Life Skills & Communication by designing a set of infographic series, "Respectful Relationships" for the students to look over and prepare themselves during their Sports For Life (SFL) and internship programmes. I opted for a trendy lineart style to make the information more engaging for the students to read and digest.
