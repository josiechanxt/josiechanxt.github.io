Pizza Hut Singapore

Advertising, ideation, storyboarding

Client: Pizza Hut Singapore

One of the clients I worked on is one of the best pizza brands to ever exist! It is none other than Pizza Hut (Singapore). This was done during my internship at Blak Labs and is my first agency client I worked for in the industry which is something memorable.

Satay Pizza Key Visual Scamps

As part of the team, I helped draw out some key visual ideas for the Pizza Hut Satay Pizza to be presented as potential options to the client.

Nasi Lemak Pizza TVC Storyboard Frames

I was in charge of drawing storyboards based on the Art Director's ideas for the Pizza Hut Nasi Lemak TVC. This was used during the production shoot as visual reference for the crew.

End result: I also had a chance to attend a production shoot at the location and witness how everything from visualisations turned into real life which was magical. My colleagues and I played the role of Art Directors in which we oversaw how everything plays out and make sure things are according to the visions envisioned (teamwork~).

Pizza Box Design Drafts (Non-Work)

I also designed some potential designs for Pizza boxes as an assignment during internship.
