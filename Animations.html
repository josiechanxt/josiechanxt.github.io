2D Motion Graphics Storyboarding

“Conceptualised and executed motion graphics for various projects (UX/UI, advertising)”

My background as a Junior Art Director in the advertising industry provided me with a strong foundation in conceptualising art direction and executing 2D motion design. I now apply these skills in my UX/UI projects, such as when designing micro-interactions, to elevate interactivity and ensure the final product is a both functional and delightful experience for the user.

    • GovTech Search Bar microinteraction

    Industry Project • 2025

    During my time at GovTech, I illustrated and animated a custom micro-interaction for an internal staff directory. To ensure fast web performance without sacrificing visual quality, I hand-drew the assets from scratch in procreate, vectorised it in Illustrator and animated them in After Effects, exporting the final result via Bodymovin (Lottiefile) as a JSON file for a scalable implementation.

    Pushing beyond my comfort zone in hand-drawn animation, I created a responsive character that reacts to user behaviour:


    1️⃣ Hover: The character turns to face the user, creating an inviting "hello" moment.

    2️⃣ Unhover: She spins back to her original position.

    3️⃣ Typing: She looks down at the search bar, as the user types.

    4️⃣ Idle: She looks back up, ready for the next interaction.


    This project has reached over 4,000 employees, teaching me how thoughtful, "quirky" details can humanise a digital interface and delight users at scale.

    "GoKakis" Interactive Projector game interface

    School Project • 2025

    GoKakis is an interactive projection system with an old-school charm that turns the long waiting time of elderly’s rehabilitation appointments into an opportunity of social interaction and fun, while gamifying physiotherapy. Housed in a compact, A4-sized projector, the system turns any floor into a 'playground of memories' featuring nostalgic Singaporean games like “Chapteh” and “Longkang Fishing”. Our goal is to foster spontaneous play and reduce staff dependency to set up equipment.

    In this project, I led the art direction, designed the game interface, and animated the interactive game elements to ensure a seamless, engaging experience for seniors.

    Singlife Logo Animation

    Maybank Anti-Scam TVC

    Industry Project • 2022

    I layout the storyboard and animated a 30-second video for Maybank to educate their customers on how to avoid scams. Since it was information-heavy, I focused on pacing the animation such that it was easily digestable.

    Social Media Posts

    Industry Project • 2022

    I also had experience animating social media content for global brands like Red Bull and Yoyic. Each post took around 30 minutes to 1.5 hours of animation time.

    For Red Bull, I was given static Photoshop and Illustrator assets that I was tasked with turning into animations. By deconstructing the artwork into individual layers and thinking in frames, I created fast-paced, impactful motion that aligned with the brand's high-energy, powerful identity. I learnt the importance of pacing the animation as it needs to be under 10 seconds to capture the viewer’s attention, while still ensuring the clarity of copy for smooth reading.

    Additionally, my work for Yoyic involved a more end-to-end creative process. I brainstormed the storytelling of the posts and created vector illustrations that matched the brand’s flat graphic aesthetic before bringing them to life through animation.
