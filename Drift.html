Drift Night Lamp

Product Design

School Project • 2024

Problem

Nighttime offers a refuge from our busy days, but restlessness often keeps us awake, craving moments lost to daily demands. This highlights the need for a more fulfilling sleep experience.

Solution

Inspired by this, we created a sleep aid that guides users into natural sleep and wake cycles. Through surveys and interviews, we found that ambient sounds and lighting improve sleep quality. Drawing from nature, we focused on the calming rhythm of ocean waves to enhance bedtime routines.

Product

Introducing Drift: a night lamp that projects soothing, wave-like patterns in your bedroom through light and sound. Its hourglass-shaped body, crafted from glossy black acrylic, mimics ocean fluidity. Vibrational speakers create wave patterns on a water-filled membrane, which are projected onto the ceiling with soft light. A companion app allows remote control and customisation of lighting, sound, fade effects, and timers.
